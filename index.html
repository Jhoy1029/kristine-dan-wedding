The Beginning

It all began in 2016, when two teenagers met as classmates in senior high school. He was the quiet boy with a guitar, and she was the girl who loved to sing. Though they didn't talk much at first, a school performance brought them together—he played, she sang, and something beautiful began to bloom.

Growing Together

What started as a simple friendship soon grew into something more. Their connection was effortless—filled with laughter, shared moments, and a bond that deepened over time. One day, she confessed her feelings, and though shy and unexpected, he had already felt the same way.

Journey Begins

As teenagers, they once held a mock wedding—dressed in white, sharing vows over dinner, and dreaming of a future together. That night, a silent prayer was made: that this love would last a lifetime.

Love Grows

Their love continued to blossom through the challenges of growing up together. Every moment shared strengthened their bond and deepened their commitment to each other.

Distance & Growth

They journeyed through love, growth, distance, and rediscovery. Despite the challenges, their love only grew stronger, proving that true love can withstand any obstacle.

A Beautiful Blessing

Their love story welcomed a new chapter with the arrival of their beautiful child, Kayelannoir. This precious blessing brought even more joy and meaning to their journey together.

Forever Begins

Now, nine years later—with a beautiful child and a love that's stood the test of time—they are finally turning that dream into reality. This is the forever they always believed in.

Important Reminders

Please take note of these important details for our special day

I. Please Stay Until the End

We humbly ask that you stay for the entire event, which will last approximately 3–4 hours including the ceremony and reception. We truly appreciate your presence and hope you'll celebrate this special day with us from beginning to end.

II. Unplugged Ceremony

During the wedding ceremony, we kindly request that all cellphones and gadgets be put away. Our professional photographers will be capturing every beautiful moment — including you! So relax, be present, and enjoy the ceremony.

III. Dress Code

To match the theme, we encourage everyone to wear vintage or classic attire. Kindly avoid T-shirts, shorts, tank tops, or any modern casual wear. Let's dress to impress — timelessly!

IV. For Guests with Children

If you're bringing children, we kindly ask that they remain seated and refrain from running around to maintain the flow and respectfulness of the event.

V. Guest List Policy

As much as we'd love to celebrate with everyone, we can only accommodate those listed on our guest list. We kindly ask that no unannounced or additional guests be brought. Your understanding is deeply appreciated.
